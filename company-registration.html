Choosing the right legal entity for your company

Sole Proprietorship

  • Simplest business structure
  • Owner has unlimited liability
  • No legal distinction between owner and business
  • Easy to establish and dissolve

Partnership

  • Two or more co-owners
  • Shared profits and liabilities
  • Partnership agreement recommended
  • Several partnership types available

Limited Liability Company (LLC)

  • Personal asset protection
  • Flexible management structure
  • Pass-through taxation
  • Popular choice for small businesses
